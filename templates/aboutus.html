Welcome to Parammpara

Authentic Indian Vegetarian Cuisine | Family Ambience | Trusted Quality

Experience the Taste of Tradition

Parammpara is a celebration of India’s timeless culinary heritage. We are a pure vegetarian family restaurant committed to delivering authentic flavors using traditional recipes and modern hygiene practices.

  • 100% Pure Vegetarian Multi-Cuisine
  • Fresh Thalis, Snacks, and Sweets
  • South/North Indian, Chinese & Tandoori
  • Comfortable AC Seating with Cultural Decor
  • Event Catering & Jain Food on Request

We serve food with love and care in a warm family setting.
Join us to relive the flavors of tradition!
